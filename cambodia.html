Cambodia cuisine

Amok Trey

A national dish made from fish, coconut milk, and a curry paste called kroeung, steamed in banana leaves.

Khmer Red Curry

A less spicy coconut-milk-based curry with beef, chicken, or fish, potatoes, and lemongrass.

Nom Banh Chok

A beloved breakfast soup consisting of rice noodles topped with a fish-based green curry gravy.

Cambodia culture

Bayon Temple

Bayon is in the heart of the ancient city of Angkor Thom, which was the symbolic center of the Khmer empire.

Khmer Housing

Traditional Khmer housing refers to the construction of houses by the Khmer people since the ancient time and evolved until today.

Royal Ballet

The Royal Ballet of Cambodia, Classical Dance, is a revered art form that embodies the rich cultural heritage of Cambodia.

Religion

Buddhism is the predominant religion in Cambodia, 97% of the population as Buddhists, practicing Theravada Buddhism.

Best Things About Cambodia

Best Time to Visit

February to April is the best time to visit Cambodia. This season is pleasant to perfect because of Angkor Wat sunrise, Beaches and festivals .Days are dry and sunny or no rainfall.

Cambodia's diverse landscapes

Tonlé Sap Lake: A UNESCO Biosphere Reserve with floating villages.Battambang: Known for its French colonial architecture and bamboo railway.Kampot: A charming riverside town famous for its pepper plantations.

Kingdom of Cambodia

The Kingdom of Cambodia is a Southeast Asian nation known for its rich history, cultural heritage. Jayavarman II declared himself king, uniting the warring Khmer princes of Chenla under the name "Kambuja".

Culture & Festivals

Cambodia is a country rich in culture and tradition, where festivals and holidays play an integral role.The country's unique blend of Buddhist and animist beliefs is reflected in its many festivals and customs.
